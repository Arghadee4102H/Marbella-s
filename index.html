About Us

Marbella's is a cozy European inspired café located in Hindustan Park, Kolkata. Known for its comforting soul food, delicious pancakes, pizzas, burgers and signature café beverages, Marbella's offers the perfect spot for a relaxing brunch, coffee date or evening meal.

Whether you are stopping by for a quiet latte or enjoying a vibrant evening with friends, Marbella's delivers warm hospitality and a delightful dining experience.
