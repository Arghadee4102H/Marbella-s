Our Story

Cozy. Warm. European.

Marbella's is a cozy European-inspired café located in Hindustan Park, Kolkata. Known for its comforting soul food, delicious pancakes, burgers, flatbreads and global café dishes, Marbella's offers the perfect space for brunch, coffee dates and relaxed evenings.

Drop by for a quiet latte or enjoy a vibrant evening with friends in our warm and welcoming atmosphere.
